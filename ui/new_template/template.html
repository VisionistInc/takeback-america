More than 70% of those who abuse prescription pain killers get them from friends & family. In what areas should Take-Back centers be more accessible?

The Take-Back AMERICA team calculated statistics for every county in the United States to identify where prescription take-back centers are available, in comparison to the prevalence of prescription opioid abuse.
